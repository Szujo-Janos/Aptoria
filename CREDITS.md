## Product direction

The current `0.0.x` line focuses on an **evidence-first API QA and release decision workflow**.

Aptoria is not intended to clone Postman, Newman, Jira, Datadog or a full test-management suite. It is designed to collect the release-critical weak points those tools often leave scattered into a single self-hosted evidence and release decision layer while remaining usable on its own.
